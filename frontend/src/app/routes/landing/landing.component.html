Open Architecture

Our gateway device is the bridge between your farm and our platform, enabling any device, sensor or output to be monitored or controlled from anywhere around the world, regardless of protocol or data format.

The unique shim layers, coupled with our expressive frontend scripter enables cross-device communication with anything from PLCs, RTUs or off-the-shelf Arduinos. No code required.
